# Resume Bullets

## Best project title

**Azure-Ready Supply Chain Forecasting & Inventory Optimization Control Tower**  
Python, SQL, Power BI, Scikit-learn, Pandas, SQLite/Azure SQL-ready Modeling, Scenario Simulation

## Strong version

- Built an Azure-ready supply-chain control tower using Python, SQL, and Power BI-ready outputs to forecast SKU-level demand, optimize inventory policies, and simulate cost-service tradeoffs across warehouses and regions.
- Developed a relational data model for products, orders, shipments, warehouses, inventory snapshots, supplier lead times, forecast outputs, inventory recommendations, and scenario results.
- Forecasted regional SKU demand using a Random Forest model and converted predictions into safety stock, reorder point, EOQ, stock coverage, and replenishment recommendations.
- Designed scenario simulations for demand surges, supplier delays, higher service levels, shipping-cost increases, and warehouse capacity reductions to quantify stockout risk and inventory-cost impact.
- Built a Power BI-ready reporting layer with executive summary, monthly KPIs, inventory risk, scenario comparison, warehouse allocation, and forecast accuracy outputs.
